Privacy Policy of the SmartWeather Mobile App



1. Introduction
SmartWeather is committed to protecting the privacy and security of its users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use the SmartWeather Mobile App. By downloading, installing, or using the App, you consent to the terms and practices outlined in this policy.


2. Information Collection and Use
2.1 User Location Information
The SmartWeather App collects your location information to provide you with accurate and localized weather data. We utilize the GPS location service of your mobile device to determine your geographical position and deliver weather information specific to your location. We assure you that your location data is solely used for the purpose of providing personalized weather services and is not shared with any third parties without your explicit consent.
2.2 Personal Information
SmartWeather does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) unless explicitly provided by you for feedback or support purposes. We do not require users to create an account or provide personal details to use the basic functionality of the App.


3. Data Storage and Security
3.1 Data Storage
SmartWeather minimizes the storage of user data. Your location information is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers. We do not retain any historical location data beyond what is necessary to provide real-time weather updates.

3.2 Security Measures
We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. These measures include the use of encryption, secure data transmission protocols, and regular security assessments of our systems. However, please note that no method of data transmission or storage can guarantee absolute security.


4. Third-Party Services
4.1 Advertising and Analytics
SmartWeather may incorporate third-party services, such as advertising networks and analytics providers, to improve the App's functionality and user experience. These services may collect non-personal information about your device, such as device type, operating system, and usage statistics. The data collected by these services is used solely for analysis and optimization purposes and is subject to their respective privacy policies.

4.2 Links to Third-Party Websites
The SmartWeather App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites or services before providing them with your personal information.


5. Children's Privacy
SmartWeather does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with their personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove the information from our systems.


6. Changes to this Privacy Policy
SmartWeather reserves the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App or through other appropriate means. It is your responsibility to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.


7. Contact Us
If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the SmartWeather Mobile App, please contact us at dungdtiosdeveloper@gmail.com. We will strive to respond to your inquiries in a timely manner.
By using the SmartWeather Mobile App, you acknowledge and consent to the terms and practices described in this Privacy Policy.
